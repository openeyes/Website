Description:

This element records the past medical history as described in the draft Glaucoma National Dataset of the Royal College of Ophthalmologists sections 7.21 to 7.43. It will appear in the examination event, and opened by default for new glaucoma patients.

Specific conditions that require mandatory yes:no answers are listed first, and any other condition can be added by using either the drop down menu, or the incremental search. Dates are 'fuzzy' in that they can be edited with as much specificity as required (NB Both incremental search and fuzzy dates are implemented in the current release, but not in this demo).

The list of diagnoses will also appear in the patient summary page and the glaucoma management plan.

Questions for the user group include;

  1. What order should the mandatory specific conditions appear in?
  2. What should be the content of the drop down menu
  3. How should we handle the additional functions described in the dataset for heart block, IHD and DM?
